### Theme
Education | Behavioral Analytics | Personalization

### Context
Students differ widely in how they absorb information, engage in class, and respond to instruction. Teachers rarely have tools that reveal these differences at scale or suggest targeted interventions.

### Problem
Build a learning pattern analysis and guidance framework that helps educators understand student learning behaviors and adapt teaching strategies accordingly.

### What the Solution Should Enable
- Identification of learning tendencies or groups  
- Explanation of why a student fits a certain pattern  
- Early signals of disengagement or struggle  
- Teaching strategies aligned to observed patterns  

### Open Design Space
Teams may explore:
- Persona discovery or clustering  
- Feature-driven explanations  
- Class-level and school-level summaries  
- Personalized intervention suggestions  

### Evaluation Focus
- Practical usefulness for teachers  
- Interpretability and fairness  
- Creativity in instructional recommendations
